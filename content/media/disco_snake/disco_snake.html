Disco Snake by Jim Purbrick

Use arrow keys to move, eat food to add notes to sequence, eat tail to switch samples. Food on right produces louder notes, food at top produces higher notes. Press any key to begin. Share the full URL to share your tune. Click here to restart.

(Sound currently only works on browsers that support MP3 or OGG played via the HTML5 audio element (Firefox, Safari and Chrome). Even on those browsers HTML5 audio support is fragile. If you don't hear anything or don't like the glitches you're hearing, try using a different browser or reloading a few times.)
